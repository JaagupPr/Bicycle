Bicycle News, a division of Pedal Faster 2023

Welcome to Bicycle News by Pedal Faster, your ultimate destination for all things related to cycling!

At Bicycle News, we are passionate about bikes and everything associated with them. Whether you're a casual rider, a dedicated commuter, or a seasoned pro, our goal is to provide you with the latest news, insightful articles, and helpful tips to enhance your cycling experience.

Our team of avid cyclists and dedicated journalists scours the cycling world to bring you the most up-to-date information on industry trends, innovations, and events. From new bike releases and gear reviews to interviews with cycling professionals and coverage of major races, we strive to keep you informed and inspired.

Whether you're embarking on an epic bikepacking adventure, tackling a challenging mountain trail, or simply commuting to work, we want to hear from you!

So, whether you're a seasoned cyclist or just getting started, join us on this exciting journey as we explore the world of cycling together. Pedal faster, and let's ride towards a brighter, greener future!

Stay tuned to Bicycle News by Pedal Faster for all the latest updates and insights from the world of cycling. Thank you for being a part of our community!

Happy riding!
